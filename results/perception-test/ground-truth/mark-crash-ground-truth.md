# Ground Truth: Mark Crash

## 1. Rider objective & context
The rider is descending a steep hill into a jump at the bottom. Third-person filming.

## 2. Rider
Weight is back at the start of the descent. Weight stays back through the jump, which causes the rebound off the back end to transfer into his body. This overrotates him on landing and he crashes.

## 3. Terrain & environment
Shaley, rocky, steep hill with a jump at the bottom.

## 4. Events
- Rider descends a steep, shaley, rocky hill
- Keeps weight back throughout
- Hits the jump at the bottom
- Weight still back — back end rebounds into his body
- Overrotates on landing
- Crashes and appears hurt

## 5. Outcome
Crash on landing. Rider is visibly hurt.
