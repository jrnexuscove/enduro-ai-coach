# Ground Truth: Steep Hill Bail

## 1. Rider objective & context
The rider is attempting a hill climb in dense, wintery forest. POV camera attached to helmet.

## 2. Rider
Sets off and climbs a beaten path on a steep hill for about 20 seconds. As the path becomes unclear with twigs and trees blocking the way, the rider runs out of room and falls off. The bike shoots out in front of him as he reaches a tree line full of bushes.

## 3. Terrain & environment
Dense, wintery forest. Dry conditions but heavy loose leaf cover on the ground. Steep hill with a beaten path that becomes unclear higher up. Twigs, trees, and bushes obstruct the route. Very uniform colour palette — leaves on floor, leaves on trees, branches all similar tones.

## 4. Events
- Rider sets off up a beaten path on a steep hill
- Climbs for roughly 20 seconds
- Path becomes very unclear — twigs and trees in the way
- Rider runs out of room, unclear where to go
- Falls off the bike
- Bike shoots out in front toward a tree line of bushes

## 5. Outcome
Bail/fall — rider runs out of navigable path and falls off. Bike separates and goes forward.
