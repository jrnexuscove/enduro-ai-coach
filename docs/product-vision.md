# Product Vision

## Overview
RideMind is a long-term vision to create an intelligent coaching system for off-road riders that bridges the gap between raw riding data and meaningful performance improvement.

The system aims to act like a digital coach — helping riders understand what happened, why it happened, and what to do next.

---

## Core Idea
The core idea is simple:

> Turn real riding behaviour into actionable coaching insight.

This is achieved by combining:
- rider input
- video analysis
- (eventually) sensor and telemetry data

---

## The Problem at Scale
Across all levels of riding:

- Most riders do not know exactly what they are doing wrong
- Feedback is inconsistent or unavailable
- Learning is slow and based on guesswork
- Coaching is limited, expensive, or inaccessible

There is no scalable system that provides continuous, objective feedback.

---

## Vision of the Product
RideMind aims to become:

> A system that observes a ride, understands it, and coaches the rider in a meaningful way.

At its best, the system should:

- interpret rider behaviour
- identify inefficiencies and mistakes
- provide clear, practical adjustments
- accelerate skill development

---

## Long-Term Experience Goal
The ultimate experience should feel like:

1. The rider records or uploads a ride
2. The system analyses what happened
3. The rider is shown:
   - what they did
   - what went wrong
   - what to change
4. The rider applies the feedback in the next attempt

---

## Key Differentiator
The key differentiator is not just analysis, but **understanding and simulation**.

The long-term goal includes:

- reconstructing what happened in a scenario
- identifying key moments and mistakes
- generating a **virtual playback** of:
  - what likely happened
  - what should be done differently
- guiding the rider toward improvement through visual and practical feedback

---

## Evolution Path

### Phase 1 — MVP
- simple inputs
- short video clips
- basic coaching feedback

### Phase 2 — Improved Analysis
- better understanding of movement and technique
- more structured feedback
- improved consistency

### Phase 3 — Data Integration
- IMUs and sensor inputs
- combining video and telemetry
- deeper analysis of rider and bike behaviour

### Phase 4 — Advanced Coaching
- virtual playback and scenario reconstruction
- personalised coaching suggestions
- comparison against ideal technique

---

## Expansion Opportunities
Once proven in enduro, the system can expand into:

- motocross
- mountain biking
- trials
- other skill-based outdoor sports

---

## Guiding Principle
The product should always aim to answer:

- What happened?
- Why did it happen?
- What should I do next time?

If the system answers these clearly, it is delivering real value.
---

## Advanced Vision: Avatar-Based Coaching Experience

### Core Avatar System
The long-term vision transforms RideMind from a feedback tool into an immersive, personalized riding buddy experience.

**Avatar Creation:**
- AI coaching avatar with riding buddy personality that learns the user's style
- Photo-to-avatar generation: users upload pictures in riding gear to create personalized avatars
- Avatar personalities adapt to user learning preferences and riding goals

### Interactive Video Analysis & Demonstration
**Seamless Video-to-Animation Coaching:**
- Avatar-narrated video playback that pauses at key technique moments
- Real-time transitions: actual riding footage → 3D animated scene → back to video
- Avatar demonstrates correct technique in the exact same scenario/terrain
- Split-screen comparisons: "You did this" vs "Try this instead"
- Interactive timeline with hotspots showing improvement opportunities

**Progressive Technique Breakdown:**
- Multi-layered coaching: technique → mistake identification → correction → practice drill
- "Replay this section" with avatar showing multiple approach options
- Context-aware narration ("Notice how your weight shifts here...")
- Integration with expert rider demonstrations for advanced techniques

### Immersive Learning Environment
**Virtual Coaching Scenarios:**
- 3D environment rendering of actual riding terrain from user videos
- Avatar-guided technique practice in virtual scenarios matching real rides
- Progressive skill challenges with personalized avatar feedback
- Side-by-side avatar comparisons showing user progress over time

**Social & Gamification:**
- Compare avatar progress and techniques with other riders
- Virtual group rides where avatars practice techniques together
- Achievement system tracked through avatar development
- Community challenges and technique sharing between rider avatars

### Technical Integration
This advanced system builds on the core AI coaching validation, requiring:
- 3D scene reconstruction from video footage
- Advanced motion capture and avatar animation
- Seamless video/animation rendering pipeline
- Persistent avatar learning and personalization systems

This vision positions RideMind as the first truly immersive, personalized off-road riding education platform, moving beyond traditional video analysis into interactive coaching experiences.
